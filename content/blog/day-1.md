### Why?

I am the first to reckognize that starting a blog is somewhat cringe but I am using it to better myself so it does not matter.

The reason for starting this is rooted partially in Absurdist Philosphy. When I was getting into that kind of stuff I had a hard time wrapping my head around it so here is my bad explination.

Ask any question, for example, why do I want to date this girl? Then keep asking why:

Because I like her.
Why?
Because I enjoy her presence.
Why?
Because she is smart, funny, and knows about trains.
Why?
Because her previous experiences.
Why?
I don't know.

Eventually you have to accept that there is always an "I don't know" at the end of every chain. This sorta proves that nothing truely has reason. The human perception of this is called "Absurdity" by Albert Camus.

He said there are three ways to respond to this. 
1. Religion: If you are able to find meaning within a higher power or some other kind of spirituality that is awesome and valid. You have found reason.
2. Suicide: This is a bad idea and basically claiming that a world without reason is too hard to live in.
3. Angry Acceptance: The last way is to accept that sure the world might have no reason and no meaning but fuck that. The universe might have set you up for failure, by being a being seeking meaning in a meaningless world, but you can rebel. You can do shit you enjoy in spite of that.

For me that is what this blog will help me do, answer the question "How am I going to rebel tommorow and how did I rebel today"?
