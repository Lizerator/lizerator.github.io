## Thanksgiving Recap
So I'm in NYC for thanksgiving because my mom is in the parade which is pretty fun. I like new york until I get overstimulated and have to sit in a small room for a half hour. Other than that its pretty chill.

Me and my brother didn't get signed up for the parade participants thanksgiving dinner so me and him were on our own for dinner. We decided that sitting in the hotel was a lame idea but not a lot of places were open because of the holiday.

We ended up getting an entire pie of dollar pizza hoping that we'd meet some cool people randomly and share a quick meal. We thought perchance the subway would have someone but our luck was quite dry. We ended up thinking that Washington Square Park would have some NYU students to talk too so we took the Q a couple stops downtown from somewhere around 50th street. I got a little excited and made me and my brother get off the subway one stop early (it was union square) where we ran into an unhoused fellow asking for cash.

Unfortunately we had no cash on us but offered him a slice of our pizza but he was lactose intolorant. After leaving the station it took me a second to realize that we were in the wrong place and had to turn around. On our way back we ran into a different unhoused fellow asking for cash, luckily for him we had another slice of pizza and he didn't have lactose intolerance. We got back on the subway but this time I chose the wrong line, and we ended up getting off just below SoHo. While exiting this station we found a guy playing his guitar in a funky sort of style so we danced a little jig before leaving. He didn't have venmo so I just left him my email and told him to hit me up.

Once on the street me and my brother decided on a new task, walking all the way to the empire state buildnig, which we could see directly down the street from us (like sixty or so blocks). On our way there I decided to ring my boy william and we chatted for a bit before me and my brother gave up and went back into the subway.

The final quest for the night, after so many previous failed ones, was to navigate back to our hotel in times square without the use of our phones. This meant only on the wall subway maps. I did it perfectly, boarding the right train in the right direction (the local N going uptown). Soon we were back in the hotel and watching Neon Genisis Evangelion.

At some point I grew hungry so we ventured out once again twoards a market on our block that we have gone to every night for snacks. It is located below a deli sort of place and is called "The Amish Market" I think. I picked up a big bag of peanut m&m's and one of those sparkling cider bottles. On our way back to the hotel I realized the bottle was not a twist off so we stopped by the front desk for a bottle opener. A guy helped us in opening it but it ended up exploding everywhere and everything got sticky. Not my brightest moment. Four hours later back in the hotel room I was able to get this website to work and wrote this little diddy before going to sleep. 

Overall it was a pretty chill day but I'm excited to get back to Alameda tommorow!!
